Approach
Philosophy

I build the way I operate: outcome-first, systems-minded. Every project here started as a real problem — a nonprofit needing donor tools, a small business needing leads, a traveler needing price alerts.

Why Claude

Anthropic's models are my tool of choice because they reason carefully and integrate cleanly. I've deployed Claude in chat widgets, agents, and automation pipelines — not demos, but live production tools.

Stack Philosophy

Lightweight, composable, cloud-native. GitHub Actions for orchestration. Playwright for browser automation. Supabase and Salesforce for data. Built to run without babysitting.

Operator Lens

My background is enterprise GTM and renewals at scale. I bring that lens to every build: what's the workflow, what's the failure mode, what's the metric that tells you it's working?
